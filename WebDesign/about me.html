ABOUT ME

Creative Student

Hello, my name is David Loh. I'm 61 years old and already retired. Now, I'm focusing all my energy and life on managing YL Recreation Park. I was born in Perlis and grew up here too. I'am married and succesful as an entrepreneur who manages YL Recreation Park.

My Passion

My purpose in building this place is to allow everyone to relax and spend their free time in this beautiful and unique place. Not only that, I also want to give people the opportunity to enjoy things that are only available abroad in this park. This is because many of the equipment and designs in this park were created through my inspiration while traveling in various countries. So, I hope everyone can spend their free time here comfortably and happily.
