Welcome to YL Recreation Park

YL Recreation Park is one of the best family recreation parks that provides fun, exciting, and unforgettable experiences for visitors. This park is suitable for all ages, including children, teenagers, and adults. Visitors can enjoy beautiful scenery, outdoor games, and many interesting activities together with family and friends.

Introduction

YL Recreation Park was created to everyone to fill their free time and also relax themselve. This park is very spacious with an area of 3 acres.This park has been open for one year and has been visited by 2000 customers. Operating hours are Monday to Sunday but except Tuesday. The public holidays and school holidays open as well. Daily operating hours are from 10.00a.m to 7.00p.m. This park is not just an ordinary park it has many activities and games that can be carried out here. All the things have been provided for visitors to play with the most important people to create beautiful memories together.

Fun Activities

The owner of YL Recreation Park has planted many sweet and fragrant mango trees here. The owner allows all visitors to pick mangoes for free here.

This park also has tents that have been set up, so anyone who wants to camp can come here.

The park also has a small house called "Laman Sehati" to allow visitors to color cute toy doll and make handicrafts here.

YL Recreation Park also provides a swimming pool for children to play in the pool and swim there during the summer.

The owner of this park also provides game equipment such as badminton rackets and badminton shuttlecocks so that visitors can play together with their families.

Apart from that, places and equipment such as bows and arrows are also provided for customer to do archery activities so that visitors can experience archery.>

In this park there is also a small pond with many floating duck doll, so it allows visitors to play a duck catching game to strengthen their relationships with each other.

Uniqueness of the Park

YL Recreation Park looks unique compared to other parks because it has a cafe for customer to rest if they are feeling tired or hot. There are many food and drinks in this cafe that customer can choose from.

YL Recreation Park also has many beautiful places, including 'Denal Flora'. This place is perfect for visitors to take pictures, especially the lovers.

The park also has a place where visitors can experience the fun of entering a labyrinth. This place is suitable for both children and adults.

The owner of YL Recreation Park has also built a homestay for visitors to stay in. The total cost to stay overnight is RM100.

The park also has a mini Flora's Red Ferris Wheel which was the owner's own idea when he was traveling abroad. Visitors can take pictures with this wheel.

Although many parks already have fish ponds, this park is different because visitors can feed the fish themselves.

This park also has the which has the 'Gerbang Impian' meaning of various dreams because it's colorful. This place is also suitable for visitors to taking pictures.

This place is specially designed for children because they can create their own crafts here. This place can create the best memories for the children.
