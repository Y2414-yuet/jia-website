MY EXPERIENCE

Before becoming the owner of YL Recreation Park, I had many years of work experience. The work experience I have gained over those years can help me manage the park more easily.

01

Work History

Before I was not the owner of YL Recreation Park, I had worked in different positions and companies. Among the jobs I had done was being a normal worker at a bank. After a long time I worked from the lowest rank in the bank, my effort finally paid off, which was I was promoted to become a bank manager. During this period of time, I worked at the bank for 18 years. After that I started the import and export business. Later, I started to create my own business which is developing YL Recreation Park. Before this park opened, I had traveled all over the country to do research and get ideas to design this park, so that this park would be more unique and memorable for many people.

02

Job Scope & Responsibilities

I'm the founder and owner of YL Recreation Park. However, as the owner, I'm also involved in managing this park. This is because I will always come to this park to make sure the safety and environment of this park are clean and safe for visitors. In addition, I will also lead and introduce visitors to the interesting places in this park, so that they don't miss any interesting places or games.

03

Challenges

During my time managing YL Recreation Park, I have faced several challenges. Among the challenges is that customers decrease according to certain seasons, for example during the rainy season or weekdays. In addition, the location of this park is in an interior area, which has caused many people to miss the opportunity to visit this place. Not only that, this park is also difficult to manage due to a lack of workers. Finally, the fish in the pond are often eaten by animals such as owls and storks. Small animals such as turtles are often lost because they run away from their place or are stolen by visitors. Playground equipment in the park is also often damaged due to the carelessness and rudeness of visitors.
